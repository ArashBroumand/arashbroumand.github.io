The Plague

Two hundred and thirty two days of quarantine has not got into me yet, or maybe it has and I am in denial. I do not know for sure. On Wednesday, February 19th, 2019, before leaving the laboratory, I instructed our members to take extraordinary measures, take a leave of absence for a week or two, take good care of themselves and their family. I did not have the slightest idea how long it was going to last and how much is going to change in our lives...
